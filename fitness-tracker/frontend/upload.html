📱 Mi Fitness (Redmi Watch)

How to export your data:

  1. 1Open the Mi Fitness app on your phone
  2. 2Tap Profile (bottom right)
  3. 3Tap the Settings icon (top right, ⚙️)
  4. 4Tap Account & Privacy → Export Personal Data
  5. 5Tap Export — Mi Fitness will prepare a ZIP and download it
  6. 6Transfer the ZIP to your computer, or open this page from your phone and upload directly
🏃 Runkeeper

How to export your data:

  1. 1Open the Runkeeper app or go to runkeeper.com
  2. 2Go to Settings → Export Data
  3. 3Tap Request Data Export — Runkeeper emails you a download link
  4. 4Download the ZIP from the email link and upload it below
